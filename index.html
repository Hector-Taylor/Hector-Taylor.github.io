About

Hector Taylor is a researcher and builder in Zurich.

Hector Taylor works across neuroscience, psychedelic research, virtual reality, embodiment and neuroimaging, with a current focus on chronic pain and body representation.

This site is the canonical home for Hector Taylor online. It will collect research updates, projects, writing, publications, tools and experiments as they become ready to share.

Research

Altered states, bodies and measurement.

  • Psychedelics and virtual reality for chronic pain

    Research interest in therapeutic approaches that combine altered states with immersive body-based interventions.

  • Embodiment and body representation

    Work on how sensory illusions, virtual bodies and cross-modal cues can change how people perceive and inhabit their bodies.

  • Neuroimaging and systemic physiology

    Interest in systemic physiology-augmented functional near-infrared spectroscopy and related biosignal methods.
